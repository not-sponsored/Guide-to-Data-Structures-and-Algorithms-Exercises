# chapter 5 - Using Big O 


1. The time complexity of 4N + 16 steps is O(N).

2. The time complexity of 2N^2 is O(N^2).

3. The time complexity of two separate traversals of an array is O(N). 

4. The time complexity of a single traversal of an array is O(N).

5. The time complexity of nested for loops is O(N^2).
